The Guide Too The Land Of Ooo

Candy Kingdom

Come Visit


  1. Princess Bubblegum's Castle: The central castle and primary residence of Princess Bubblegum.

  2. Candy Tavern: A prominent establishment, often seen as a central building or location.

  3. Candy Kingdom Mental Hospital: A separate castle-shaped building for mental patients.



The Fiery Fire Kingdom

Come Visit


  1. Fire Palace: The royal castle and seat of power, home to the Flame King and Flame Princess.

  2. Fire Kingdom Core: The kingdom's power core, which is vital to its operation.

  3. Firebreak Island: An island region near the Fire Kingdom, that is made of hardened lava and dominated by a volcano.



The Ice Kingdom

Come Visit


  1. The Ice King's Castle a large, hollowed-out ice structure located in the middle of the Ice Kingdom, which is a frigid land populated mostly by penguins. The castle's interior is chaotic and reflects the Ice King's personality, featuring a mix of furnished rooms, secret chambers, and dungeons, alongside piles of trash and ice and snow-made items. Key features include a bedroom with a fancy bed, a drum set, and gym equipment, a kitchen, and a cage for holding princesses captive.
